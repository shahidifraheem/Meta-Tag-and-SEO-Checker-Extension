Privacy Policy for Meta Tag & SEO Checker Chrome Extension

Last Updated: 06/19/2025

1. Information We Collect

The Meta Tag & SEO Checker extension ("the Extension") collects and processes the following information:

  • Page Content Analysis: The Extension analyzes the HTML content of web pages you visit to extract meta tags and SEO-related information.
  • URL Information: The Extension collects URLs of pages you analyze to provide historical tracking of SEO scores.
  • Local Storage: The Extension stores your analysis history locally in your browser's storage for your convenience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Providing SEO analysis and recommendations
  • Displaying historical SEO score trends
  • Generating exportable reports

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by the Extension is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers. Your data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.

4. Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following permissions:

  • "activeTab": To access the content of the currently active tab for analysis
  • "scripting": To inject the content script needed for analysis
  • "storage": To save your analysis history locally in your browser
